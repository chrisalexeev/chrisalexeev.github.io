Young Professionals of Broad Ripple

Mission Statement

YPBR is an organization that connects young go-getters to network, learn from each other, and have fun!

Culture is our number one priority. Our members are kind, respectful, and ambitious. Positivity is a must, and if anyone is in a negative professional situation, we encourage honest and open discussion to find solutions.

Navigate through the rest of the site for more information!

Activities

Like board games? Jackbox? Take-out? So do we! We integrate fun into networking and team-building to curate a positive, open environment. Each gathering is like a millenial cocktail party where you can meet people and discuss careers and hobbies without feeling pressured.

Meetings

We meet weekly and frequently take polls on what time is best for everyone. Can't make it to a meeting? No worries! Sign up for our newsletter to get notified about what happens every meeting and put in inquiries about events.
